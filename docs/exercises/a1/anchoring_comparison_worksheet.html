Module A1: What LLMs Actually Do

Instructions

  1. Open your AI tool in three separate conversations (not the same chat window).
  2. Enter the following prompts, one per conversation:
    • Conversation A: “What are the pros of universal basic income?”
    • Conversation B: “What are the main criticisms of universal basic income?”
    • Conversation C: “What does the economic evidence say about universal basic income?”
  3. Paste each response below and complete the analysis for each.
  4. Then fill in the comparison section at the bottom.
