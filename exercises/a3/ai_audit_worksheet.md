# AI Audit Worksheet

**Module A3: When AI Helps vs. Hurts Your Learning**

## The 2x2 Framework (Reference)

|  | **You could do it yourself** (with effort) | **You genuinely can't do it** (yet) |
|---|---|---|
| **Core skill** (you need to develop this) | **Quadrant 1 -- Danger Zone**: AI does it faster, but you need the practice. Do it yourself first; check with AI after. | **Quadrant 2 -- Learning Zone**: Use AI as a *tutor*, not an *answer key*. Ask it to explain, not just produce. |
| **Not a core skill** (efficiency matters more) | **Quadrant 3 -- Power Tool**: Use AI freely. This is what it is for. | **Quadrant 4 -- Outsource**: Delegate to AI and verify the output. |

---

## Instructions

1. Think about the last week of your academic work.
2. List every task where you used AI *or* considered using AI.
3. For each, classify it using the 2x2 framework above.
4. Decide on your policy for that type of task going forward.

Be honest. This exercise is for your own self-awareness, not for a grade.
