## Section 3: My Rules

*Concrete commitments I am making about how I use AI this semester.*

Check the rules you are adopting, and add your own:

- [ ] I will always attempt a task for at least **10 minutes** on my own before asking AI.
- [ ] I will always **verify citations** and factual claims produced by AI.
- [ ] I will use AI as a **tutor** (explain concepts, check my work) rather than an **answer key** (generate the answer for me).
- [ ] When AI generates code for me, I will **read and understand every line** before using it.
- [ ] I will not paste homework questions directly into AI without first attempting them myself.
- [ ] I will keep a **prompt log** to track how I am using AI and what I am learning from it.
